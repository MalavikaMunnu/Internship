MUSICAL INSTRUMENTS

Musical instruments are devices created or adapted to make musical sounds.


VIOLIN

The violin, sometimes know as fiddle, is a wooden chrodophone in the violin family. Most violins have a hollow wooden body.




FLUTE

The flute is a member of the family of musical instruments in the woodwind group. Like all woodwinds, flutes are aeroplanes,producing sound with a vibrating column of air.


VEENA

The veena encompasses various chrodophone instruments from the Indian subcontinent. The many regional designs have different names such as the Rudra veena, the Saraswati veena, the Vichitra veena and others.

PIANO

The piano is a keyboard instrument that produces sound when pressed on keys. When keys are pressed, the tightened strings inside are struck by coated wooden hammers.





DRUMS

The drum is a member of the percussion group of musical instruments. In the Hornbostel-Sachs classification, it is a membranophone. Drums are the world's oldest and most ubiquitous musical instruments. A number of different drums together with cymbals form the basic modern drum kit.
